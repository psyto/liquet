LIQUET. live demo · self-contained · no rpc

Cross-VM settlement verification

Bridges move tokens and hope. Liquet re-executes both legs and proves the intent was honored.

A chain-abstract intent settles across two chains — an EVM pay-leg and a Solana delivery-leg. Liquet re-runs both in-process, reconciles them against what the solver actually promised, and answers one question before money is released: did this settle the way it was supposed to?

View the code on GitHub ↗seam + gate build standalone · Apache-2.0

The bridge model

Move, then trust

Tokens leave one chain and (you hope) arrive on another. When one side lands and the other doesn't, you're holding an in-flight position and a support ticket.

The Liquet model

Re-execute, then prove

Both legs are replayed deterministically and reconciled to the intent. Funds release only on a Matched verdict — half-settled and mis-delivered outcomes are caught, not discovered later.

02

How the gate decides

Two independent producers, one decision. No single component both executes and judges.

re-exec

Replay both legs

EVM leg in revm, SVM leg in LiteSVM — deterministic, from supplied prestate. Each leg's facts are recovered, not asserted.

reconcile

Bind to intent

Recovered recipient, amount, asset and settlement-id on both legs are checked against the claim. Verdict: Matched / HalfOpen / Mismatch.

screen

Invariant check

The delivery leg is screened for malice (drain, delegate, authority change) by an independent producer — the safety slot.

gate

Settle or hold

Release only on Matched and a clean screen. Every hold reports exactly why funds were withheld.

03

What it proves today — and what's next

The verification is real and self-contained. The parts that aren't finished are named, not hidden — that's the point of a verifier.

Proven now

  • ✓Both legs re-executed in-process — revm + LiteSVM, zero network, zero fixtures.
  • ✓A Matched verdict is real value-binding: recipient, amount and settlement-id recovered and checked against intent.
  • ✓Half-open and mis-delivery held before settlement — the failures bridges surface too late.
  • ✓Proof and screen come from two independent producers — no common-mode blind spot.
  • ✓Slot 2 catches what reconcile can't — a backdoored delivery that reconciles Matched is held by the independent Custos screen (F2-delegate), replayed on probatio's exact tx. Not a stub.
